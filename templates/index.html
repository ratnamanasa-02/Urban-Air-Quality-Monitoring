Urban Air Quality



Urban air quality refers to the quality of air in urban areas, which is influenced by various sources

of pollution such as vehicle emissions, industrial activities, and construction.

Poor urban air quality can have detrimental effects on human health, leading to respiratory problems,

cardiovascular diseases, and other health issues.

To improve urban air quality, it is essential to implement measures such as promoting the use of public

transportation, enforcing strict emission standards for vehicles and industries, increasing green spaces,

and raising awareness about air pollution and its impacts on health and the environment.
